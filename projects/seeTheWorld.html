See The World

About this project:

This was a group project, me and my partner decided to build something that any person will love as soon as they see it. So we decided to build an app where travelers can share articles about those beautiful places that you visit when you go on a trip.

An app to share travel experiences with exciting photos, tips on traveling to those locations, and sharing stories. You can click an article and see the information about that place, including the author and a map that shows where exactly is that place. To see the deployed version of the project click here.
