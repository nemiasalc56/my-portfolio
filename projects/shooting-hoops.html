Shooting Hoops

About this project:

I wanted to build a game that reminds me my childhood, I used to play this game with my friends any day for many hours and have a lot of fun.

This is a two players game, when you first open the game, you will see a square with the instructions on how to play the game. This is a 4 rounds game or more. The player that scores more points in each round will win that round. The player that wins the most rounds wins the game. To see the deployed version click here.
