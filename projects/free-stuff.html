About this project:

This is an app where someone that wants to get rid of something and wish to give it to someone, they can just post it on the app with a pick up address for the user that would like that item.

The main idea behind this, is that there are many people that in their house might have many things that they don't need anymore, and they would like to give it someone. There are also many people needed everywhere, they don't have the money to buy some of the things that they need. To see the deployed version of the project click here, too see the code click here.
